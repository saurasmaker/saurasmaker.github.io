My Projects

Escape Room Video Game in VR (Virtual Reality) - Bachelor Final Project Computer Engineering

15/12/2021 – 15/05/2022


Currently, virtual reality (VR) technology is booming in popularity. Furthermore, one of the most profitable businesses today is the video game industry. This is why the project in which this TFG is developed consists of the development of a VR video game with a work team. The reason for the interest in this project will be seen; VR technology will be put into context by making a historical summary of its predecessors; The different uses that VR has beyond video games will be explained; The concepts of mixed reality, augmented reality and XR will be explained, as well as the elements necessary for their existence; A comparative study will be carried out in which it will be explained what technologies can be used and which have been used for this project; A game design document (GDD) will be presented to explain the decisions made when developing the video game in question; The methodology used and what has been worked on over time will be explained; The different techniques will be shown to check the correct operation of the software; and the final conclusions will be presented.
